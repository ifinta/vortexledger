We planning to have "channels", to evaluate a node of Vortexledger after each "appointment" between nodes:

 - RELIABILITY
 - SUSTAINABILITY
 - SIMPLICITY
 - FLEXIBILITY
 - QUALITY
 - AGILITY

We will use PoA: Proof of Appointment - as a motor for our Ledger.
We think offline first and local. No big trips needed then, and your node and your partner node can use post or something trustable delivery form also.
But every pertnership must be established with a real appointment or a trusted third party, a dealer i.e.
